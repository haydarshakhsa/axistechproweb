What types of CCTV systems do you install?

We install all major CCTV systems — including Analog, HD, IP, and Wireless cameras. Whether it’s for a home, retail shop, office, or large facility, we design a system tailored to your security needs and budget.

+

Can I view my cameras remotely on my phone or computer?

Yes! We configure your CCTV system for remote access through secure apps or web portals, so you can monitor your property from anywhere, anytime.

+

How long do CCTV recordings stay saved?

It depends on your storage size and camera settings. Typically, a 1TB DVR/NVR can store 15–30 days of video for 4 cameras. We can customize storage to your preferred duration.

Do you provide maintenance or troubleshooting for existing CCTV systems?

Absolutely. We offer repair, upgrade, and maintenance services for both new and existing systems — even if they weren’t originally installed by us.
